Love Green Nepal (LGN) established in 1991.

Love Green Nepal (LGN) is an autonomous, non-governmental development organization (NGO) established in 1991. It is a legal entity registered (Regd. No. 249/048/49) with Kathmandu District Administrative Office of Nepal Government under the Organization Registration Act of 1977. LGN is affiliated (Affiliation No. 9149) to the Social Welfare Council a coordinating and umbrella body of NGOs in Nepal. It is also registered with Inland Revenue Department, Kathmandu (PAN No.301708975).

It is an NGO run collectively by the Nepali nationals with diverse academic as well as professional expertise and background ranging from management to community development. LGN members and staffs work with a strong commitment towards contributing to upliftment of low income people living in rural communities.

What we do

The main objective is to enhance greenery development and mitigate the environmental problems comprises deforestation, landslides, drying up of natural water sources as well as to enhance the farmer’s income.

Bio-gas helps to reduce rate of deforestation and environmental degradation by providing alternative source of energy. It helps to reduce the carbon dioxide generation and reduces cooking time availing extra hours for village women.

Nepal is developing country with an agricultural economy. The main occupation of Nepal is agriculture. About 80% of people are involved in as their occupation. Agriculture is the base of Nepalese people and for Nepal.

Education is the major foundation of social development. It is impossible that the development and prosperity cannot take place without the development of education. Therefore, the Love Green Nepal has been emphasizing education development through two major programs.

Love Green Nepal supports to construct women’s cooperative buildings for conducting various training program such as capacity building, skills development, anti-violence against women, gender discrimination, health related trainings and involving them in cooperative society for their economic empowerment.

Love Green Nepal has established up-land sustainable agriculture demonstration farm in Ward No. 2 of Panchkhal Municipality, Kavre. Its main objective is to demonstrate the up-land integrated sustainable agriculture in mid-hill reason of Nepal.
